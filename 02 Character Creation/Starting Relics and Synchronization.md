At Level 1, Seekers begin with:

- **2 Relic Slots**
- **2 Relic Synchronizations** (based on Discipline)

---

## Relic Slots

Represent how many relics a character may keep attuned.

- Start with **2**
- Increase through story progression and Discipline milestones

---

## Relic Synchronization

Represents intuitive resonance with specific relic types:

- Combat  
- Utility  
- Glyph  
- Support  

Each Discipline grants **two** starting Synchronizations (see individual files).

Characters gain more at milestone levels.
