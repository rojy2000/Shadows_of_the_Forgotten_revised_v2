Follow these steps to create a Level 1 Seeker.

1. **Read the Character Overview**  
   Characters are defined by Attributes, Background, Discipline, and Relic Synchronization.

2. **Assign Attributes**  
   - Distribute **+6 points** among FIT, DEX, WIS, INT.  
   - No attribute may start higher than **+3**.

3. **Choose a Background**  
   Pick one of the 12 backgrounds.  
   Each provides a single mechanical perk tied to an Attribute.

4. **Choose a Discipline**  
   - Vanguard  
   - Skirmisher  
   - Cipher  
   - Preserver  
   
   This determines:
   - HP
   - Frontline/Backline role tendencies
   - Starting Synchronizations
   - Feature progression

5. **Take Starting Synchronizations & Relic Slots**  
   - 2 Slots  
   - 2 Synchronizations (determined by Discipline)

6. **Set Defense Values**  
   - **Health:** 10 + FIT  
   - **AC:** 10 + DEX + Armor  

7. **Choose Starting Gear**  
   Based on Discipline.

8. **Record Advancement Rules**  
   Level-ups are milestone based and grant new stats, relic slots, and Discipline features.
