Starting gear is determined by your Discipline.

## Vanguard
- Martial melee weapon (1d8)  
- Optional shield (+1 AC)  
- Medium or Heavy armor  
- Travel kit

## Skirmisher
- Light or Martial weapon  
- Light or Medium armor  
- Utility kit

## Cipher
- Light weapon  
- Light armor  
- Glyph reader’s kit

## Preserver
- Light weapon  
- Light armor  
- Medic’s pouch
