Seekers level up through **story milestones**, not XP.

---

## Leveling Benefits

When gaining a level:

- **+1 Attribute** (every even-numbered level)
- **+5 HP + FIT**
- Relic Slots increase at designated milestones
- Disciplines grant new features at Levels **1, 3, 5, 8**

---

## Health & Defense

- **Health:** 10 + FIT at Level 1, +5 + FIT each level  
- **AC:** 10 + DEX + Armor
