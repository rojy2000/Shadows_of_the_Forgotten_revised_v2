
**Primary Role:** Support  
**Secondary Role:** Controller

Battlefield medics who anchor the party’s morale and recovery.

---

## Level 1 Features

**Steady Hands:** Aid revives allies to **2 HP** instead of 1.  
**Swift Aid:** Aid costs **1 Action** (instead of 2) when restoring 1 HP.

---

## Level 3 — Choose One

**Restorative Pulse:** Healing an ally grants them +1 AC until next turn.  
**Resolve Reinforcement:** Allies in your row get +1 to WIS saves.  
**Efficient Healer:** Your healing restores +1 HP + WIS.

---

## Level 5

**Preserver’s Rhythm:** Once/encounter, when an ally drops to 0 HP, they drop to 1 HP instead.

---

## Level 8 — Choose One

**Rallying Surge:** Once/encounter, all allies regain 2 HP.  
**Guardian’s Calm:** Allies gain +1 AC while you remain conscious.  
**Last Light:** An ally who would drop to 0 HP drops to 1 instead (once/encounter).

---

## Relic Synchronization

**Starting:** Support, Utility  
**Level 5:** +1 Support

---

## Starting Gear

- Light weapon  
- Light armor  
- Medic’s pouch  

---

## Favored Attributes
**Primary:** Wisdom  
**Secondary:** Intelligence or Fitness
