
**Primary Role:** Striker  
**Secondary Role:** Frontline

Agile fighters who exploit openings and reposition constantly.

---

## Level 1 Features

**Tactical Footwork:** After you hit an enemy, switch rows for free once per turn.  
**Exploit Opening:** +1 damage vs debuffed, stunned, disoriented, or controlled enemies.

---

## Level 3 — Choose One

**Evasive Reflexes:** +1 AC in Backline.  
**Killer’s Rhythm:** After hitting the same enemy twice, gain Advantage vs that enemy.  
**Opportunist’s Instinct:** +1 damage after any enemy switches rows.

---

## Level 5

**Battle Instincts:** Once/encounter, reroll one missed attack.

---

## Level 8 — Choose One

**Death’s Tempo:** After defeating an enemy, +2 damage on next attack.  
**Phantom Step:** Once/round, when an attack misses you, switch rows free.  
**Razor Precision:** +1 to hit permanently.

---

## Relic Synchronization

**Starting:** Combat, Utility  
**Level 5:** +1 Combat

---

## Starting Gear

- Light or Martial weapon  
- Light or Medium armor  
- Utility kit

---

## Favored Attributes
**Primary:** Dexterity  
**Secondary:** Fitness or Intelligence
