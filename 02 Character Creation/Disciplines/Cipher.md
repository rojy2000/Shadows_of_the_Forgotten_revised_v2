
**Primary Role:** Controller  
**Secondary Role:** Support

Masters of patterns, glyphs, and battlefield insight.

---

## Level 1 Features

**Glyphsense:** Auto-detect glyphs, pulses, anomalies nearby.  
**Relic Adept:** +1 to Relic Handling rolls.

---

## Level 3 — Choose One

**Vector Disruption:** Enemies you target get –1 to their next attack.  
**Insightful Command:** Once/encounter, give an ally Advantage.  
**Combat Read:** At encounter start, choose an enemy; allies gain +1 to first attack against it.

---

## Level 5

**Coordinated Insight:** When an ally hits an enemy, next ally gains +1 vs that enemy this turn.

---

## Level 8 — Choose One

**Harmonic Synergy:** Allies get +1 to attack rolls for a round.  
**Predictive Loop:** Once/encounter, force enemy to reroll a successful attack.  
**Mindwave Pulse:** Allies gain +1 AC and +1 to all saves until your next turn.

---

## Relic Synchronization

**Starting:** Utility, Glyph  
**Level 5:** +1 Utility

---

## Starting Gear

- Light weapon  
- Light armor  
- Glyph reader’s kit

---

## Favored Attributes
**Primary:** Intelligence  
**Secondary:** Wisdom or Dexterity
