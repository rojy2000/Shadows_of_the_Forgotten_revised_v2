
**Primary Role:** Frontline  
**Secondary Role:** Support

Steadfast protectors who hold the line and keep allies alive.

---

## Level 1 Features

**Iron Stance:** +1 AC while in the Frontline.  
**Dutybound:** Once per encounter, when an ally would drop to 0 HP, you take half of that damage instead.

---

## Level 3 — Choose One

**Toughened Core:** +2 Max HP per level (retroactive).  
**Lineholder:** Enemies have –1 to hit you with melee attacks in Frontline.  
**Rallying Presence:** If you took damage this round, one ally gains +1 to their next roll.

---

## Level 5

**Steadfast Guard:** +1 Resistance in the Frontline.

---

## Level 8 — Choose One

**Adamant Stance:** First attack that hits you each round is reduced by 2d6.  
**Indomitable Body:** Always have Resistance 2 (3 in Frontline).  
**Shield of Many:** Once/encounter: all allies gain +1 AC and +1 Resistance until your next turn.

---

## Relic Synchronization

**Starting:** Combat, Combat  
**Level 5:** +1 Utility

---

## Starting Gear

- Martial melee weapon (1d8)  
- Optional shield (+1 AC)  
- Medium or Heavy armor  
- Travel kit

---

## Favored Attributes
**Primary:** Fitness  
**Secondary:** Wisdom or Dexterity
