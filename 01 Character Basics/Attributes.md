Characters in _Shadows of the Forgotten_ are defined in part by four core Attributes.  

These Attributes represent broad categories of physical and mental capability.

Each Attribute modifies rolls in its domain and influences combat, relic activation, and survival.

Characters begin with the following:

- Distribute **+6 points** among FIT, DEX, WIS, INT.  
- No Attribute may start higher than **+3**.

## Fitness (FIT)

Physical power, endurance, and resistance to bodily strain.

Fitness governs:
- lifting, shoving, forcing
- breaking restraints or obstacles
- bracing against physical impact
- staying conscious or resisting pain
- surviving physical hazards (crushing, exhaustion, poison)
- melee attacks with Heavy weapons

---

## Dexterity (DEX)

Speed, precision, finesse, and subtle movement.

Dexterity governs:
- dodging attacks or traps
- stealthy or silent movement
- climbing, balancing, acrobatics
- delicate or precise manipulation
- navigating unstable ground or moving platforms
- ranged attacks and finesse-based strikes
- melee attacks with Finesse weapons

---

## Wisdom (WIS)

Perception, intuition, awareness, and emotional insight.

Wisdom governs:
- noticing danger or hidden details
- reading body language or intent
- navigational instinct and survival sense
- interpreting omens or environmental clues
- resisting fear, confusion, or mental influence
- guiding or calming companions

---

## Intelligence (INT)

Logic, learning, analysis, and understanding systems.

Intelligence governs:
- deciphering glyphs or markings
- interpreting relic behavior or functions
- analyzing ancient architecture
- solving puzzles or structural problems
- identifying substances, devices, or patterns
- operating complex or unknown devices
