
When attempting an action with an uncertain outcome, the GM calls for an Attribute test.

You succeed if:
**Your roll meets or exceeds the DC.**

The GM chooses:
1. **Which Attribute applies**
2. **Any bonuses or penalties**
3. **The DC of the task**

Attributes never limit what characters *may attempt* â€” only how effective they are.

---

## Fitness (FIT)

Physical power, stability, and endurance.

Used to:
- force, push, lift, or break  
- brace against impact  
- resist crushing, exhaustion, or toxins  
- stay conscious  
- make melee attacks with Heavy weapons  

Example:  
> Succeed on a Fitness test **(DC 14)** to break open a jammed security door.

---

## Dexterity (DEX)

Agility, finesse, reaction time.

Used to:
- dodge traps or attacks  
- move silently or nimbly  
- climb, balance, and cross hazards  
- manipulate delicate mechanisms  
- make ranged or finesse attacks  

Example:  
> Succeed on a Dexterity test **(DC 12)** to cross the unstable walkway.

---

## Wisdom (WIS)

Perception, intuition, and awareness.

Used to:
- detect danger or hidden features  
- read intent or emotions  
- interpret signs or omens  
- resist fear or mental pressure  

Example:  
> Succeed on a Wisdom test **(DC 13)** to notice movement in the underbrush.

---

## Intelligence (INT)

Logic, memory, and pattern analysis.

Used to:
- decipher glyphs or markings  
- interpret relic behavior  
- analyze structural weaknesses  
- solve puzzles or anomalies  

Example:  
> Succeed on an Intelligence test **(DC 15)** to interpret the layered glyph pattern.
