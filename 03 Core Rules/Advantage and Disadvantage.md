
Circumstances may grant Advantage or Disadvantage to a roll.

---

## Advantage
Roll **2d20**, keep the **higher** result.

Gained from:
- superior positioning  
- clever tactics  
- relic interactions  
- situational benefits  

---

## Disadvantage
Roll **2d20**, keep the **lower** result.

Gained from:
- injuries or impairments  
- harsh terrain  
- psychic or relic interference  
- severe conditions
