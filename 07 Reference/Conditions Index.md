## Entangled
Cannot switch rows. –2 to Dexterity saves.

## Stunned
Lose 1 Action on your next turn.

## Bleeding
Take 1d4 damage at the end of each turn until healed.

## Poisoned
–1 to all rolls.

## Blinded
Attacks have Disadvantage.

## Disoriented
–1 to Intelligence tests. –2 to Initiative if persistent.

## Wounded
Recover only half HP from rests.

## Unconscious
Cannot act; auto-fail Fitness and Dexterity saves; auto-hit by melee unless ruled otherwise.

## Dying
A state reached when an Unconscious character takes further harm. Healing removes Dying.

## 0 HP State
At 0 HP, a character becomes Unconscious instead of Dying immediately.

## Reviving
Aid restores 1 HP and awakens an ally on the next turn.
