## Combat Relics (Examples)

### Force Spike
A burst of concussive force targeting the Frontline.

### Lancing Bolt
A sharp directional strike affecting enemies in a row.

### Crushing Pulse
A destabilizing shockwave that disrupts armored targets.

---

## Utility Relics (Examples)

### Binding Webcaster
Restricts enemy movement; may impose **Entangled**.

### Echo Lantern
Reveals hidden glyphs or anomalies in a radius.

### Phase Anchor
Temporarily locks an enemy or object in place.

---

## Glyph Relics (Examples)

### Amulet of Forgotten Tongues
Helps interpret symbols or forgotten markings.

### Mind’s Lattice
Provides glimpses into enemy intent or hazard patterns.

### Horizon Key
Reveals pathways, exits, or weak points.

---

## Support Relics (Examples)

### Restorer’s Touch
Heals minor wounds with focused resonance.

### Aegis Bubble
Creates a protective barrier granting **Resistance**.

### Harmonizing Bell
Calms panic, fear, or disorientation in allies.
