## Combat Relics
Focused on offense and direct battlefield impact.  
Examples include:
- unleashing force blasts  
- empowering strikes  
- debuffing enemies  
- applying frontline pressure  

## Utility Relics
Tools that manipulate environment, movement, perception, or positioning.  
Examples include:
- restricting enemy motion  
- revealing hidden features  
- altering terrain  
- enabling traversal  

## Glyph Relics
Symbolic and insight-driven devices resonating with patterns and meaning.  
Examples include:
- reading glyphs  
- interpreting anomalies  
- influencing mental states  
- amplifying understanding  

## Support Relics
Centered on healing, protection, reinforcement, and stabilization.  
Examples include:
- restoring HP  
- shielding allies  
- providing resistance  
- strengthening formation
