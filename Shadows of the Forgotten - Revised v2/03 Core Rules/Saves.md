
Some hazards or relic effects call for Saving Throws (“Saves”).

Save = **1d20 + Attribute vs DC**

You succeed if your total meets or exceeds the DC.

---

## Physical Saves

### Fitness Save (FIT)
Used to resist:
- force  
- crushing  
- toxins  
- grapples  

Example:  
> Succeed on a Fitness save **(DC 14)** to endure the collapsing tunnel.

---

### Dexterity Save (DEX)
Used to avoid:
- falling debris  
- trap triggers  
- sudden environmental hazards  

Example:  
> Succeed on a Dexterity save **(DC 12)** to evade the snapping tendril.

---

## Mental Saves

### Wisdom Save (WIS)
Used to resist:
- fear  
- illusions  
- emotional disruption  

Example:  
> Succeed on a Wisdom save **(DC 15)** to resist the panic wave.

---

### Intelligence Save (INT)
Used to resist:
- overload from glyphs  
- relic feedback  
- psychic anomalies  

Example:  
> Succeed on an Intelligence save **(DC 14)** to stabilize your mind against the glyph pulse.
