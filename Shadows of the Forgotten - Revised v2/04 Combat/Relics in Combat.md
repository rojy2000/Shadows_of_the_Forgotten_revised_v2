
Relics work like powerful abilities with special activation rules.

---

## Activating Relics

Activation requires a test:

**Roll 1d20 + Attribute**, succeed if your result meets the relic’s **DC**.

---

## Synchronization Bonus

If Synchronized with the relic:
- Add **+3** to activation rolls.

---

## Activation Results

| Result | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| 20+ | Critical Success — enhanced effect |
| 12–19 | Success — full effect |
| 6–11 | Partial effect |
| 1–5 | Critical Failure |

---

## Combat Effects

Relics may create:
- blasts  
- shields  
- force bursts  
- pulses  
- stuns  
- blinds  
- glyph effects  
- psychic disruption
