
The formation breaks if the Frontline is compromised.

---

## Causes of Broken Formation

Formation breaks if the Frontline becomes:
- Empty  
- Unconscious or Dying  
- Forcefully displaced  

---

## Effects of a Broken Formation

While formation is broken:
- Enemies gain **+1 to all attacks** for 1 round  
- Enemy melee attacks may target **any Seeker**  
- Enemy ranged attacks ignore the Backline penalty  

---

## Restoring Formation

Formation is restored when:
- A Seeker occupies the Frontline  
- The row is no longer incapacitated
