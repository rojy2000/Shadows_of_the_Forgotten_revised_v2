
On your turn, you may take **2 Actions**.

Actions include:
- Attack  
- Activate a relic  
- Switch rows  
- Aid  
- Defend  
- Use an item  
- Interact with the environment  
- Discipline abilities  
- Build Resonance  

---

## Reactions

Each character may take **1 Reaction per round**, even outside their turn.

Common Reactions include:
- Guard Ally (reduce damage to an ally in your row)  
- Counterstep (avoid being forced into Frontline)  
- Snap Shot (make a ranged attack when a foe exposes itself)  
- Relic Reflex (trigger a basic relic effect, GM approval)
