
Combat in _Shadows of the Forgotten_ is designed to be:

- fast  
- atmospheric  
- tactical  
- dangerous  
- narratively significant  

Encounters emphasize:
- environmental hazards  
- relic strain  
- diverse enemy archetypes  
- multi-stage threats  
- timeline echoes and tone  
- meaningful positioning choices  

The blobber formation supports a high-speed, high-tension combat loop without grid complexity.
