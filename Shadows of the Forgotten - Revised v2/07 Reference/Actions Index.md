## Attack
Make an attack roll using the relevant Attribute.

## Aid
Restore 1 HP to an Unconscious ally.

## Activate Relic
Attempt to use a relic by making an Attribute test against its DC.

## Switch Rows
Move between Frontline and Backline.

## Defend
Use an action to reduce or prevent harm as directed by the GM.

## Use Item
Consume or activate a non-relic object with appropriate effects.

## Interact
Perform minor environment-based tasks.

## Reaction
Use a once-per-round triggered ability such as protecting an ally or firing a snap shot.
