## Activation
The process of using a relic by making an Attribute test against its DC.

## Activation Result
One of four outcomes: Critical Success, Success, Partial Success, or Critical Failure, depending on the total roll.

## Synchronization Bonus
A +3 bonus applied to activation tests when using relics a Seeker is Synchronized with.

## Partial Effect
A reduced version of a relic’s intended effect caused by moderate failure.

## Critical Failure
A result of 1–5 on an activation roll, often causing unstable or failed output.

## Relic Slot
A space representing how many relics can be attuned at once.

## Strain
An overload or disruption resulting from relic usage. This may impose penalties or disable relic functionality.

## Relic Tier
A measure of relic complexity and power influencing activation difficulty and strain severity.
