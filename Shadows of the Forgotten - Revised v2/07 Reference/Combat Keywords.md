## Attack
A roll comparing 1d20 + Attribute to a target’s AC to deal damage.

## Melee Attack
An attack targeting the Frontline using Fitness or Dexterity depending on weapon type.

## Ranged Attack
An attack that may target either row. Attacks to the Backline take –2.

## Critical Hit
A roll of 20+ that deals double weapon dice.

## Natural 1
A roll of 1. It is not an automatic failure unless ruled otherwise.

## Damage
The amount of harm dealt, equal to weapon die + Attribute.

## Aid
An action that restores 1 HP to an Unconscious ally, allowing them to awaken next turn.

## Defend
An action used to brace or protect, depending on situational GM rulings.

## Switch Rows
An action used to move between Frontline and Backline.

## Initiative
A roll made at combat start to determine if a Surprise Round occurs.

## Surprise Round
A round in which one side acts before the other due to lack of awareness.

## Reaction
A once-per-round response such as reducing damage to an ally or triggering a relic reflex.

## Formation Integrity
The stability of Frontline and Backline roles.

## Broken Formation
A state triggered when the Frontline is empty, incapacitated, or disabled, granting enemies offensive bonuses.
