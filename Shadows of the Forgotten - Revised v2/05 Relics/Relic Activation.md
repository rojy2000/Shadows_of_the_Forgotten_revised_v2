To activate a relic, make an Attribute test against the relic’s DC.

You succeed if your roll meets or exceeds the DC.

## Synchronization Bonus
If Synchronized with a relic type:
- Add +3 to activation tests.

## Activation Results

| Result | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| 20+ | Critical Success — enhanced effect |
| 12–19 | Success — full effect |
| 6–11 | Partial effect |
| 1–5 | Critical Failure |

## Attributes for Activation
Relic type determines the Attribute used:

- Combat Relics → Fitness or Dexterity  
- Utility Relics → Dexterity or Intelligence  
- Glyph Relics → Intelligence or Wisdom  
- Support Relics → Wisdom  

A relic specifies the exact Attribute needed.
