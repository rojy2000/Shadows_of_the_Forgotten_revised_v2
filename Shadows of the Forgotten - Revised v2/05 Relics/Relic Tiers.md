Relics are grouped into Tiers representing complexity and power level.

Higher tiers:
- are harder to activate  
- impose more severe strain  
- create stronger effects  
- may require story progression to attune  

Example tiers:
- Tier 1: simple relics with direct effects  
- Tier 2: multi-mode relics  
- Tier 3: battlefield-shaping relics  
- Tier 4+: rare narrative relics of immense power
