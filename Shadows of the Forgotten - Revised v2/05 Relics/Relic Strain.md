Relic Strain represents overload, instability, or disrupted resonance.

Strain may cause:
- Disadvantage on next activation  
- Weakened or reduced effects  
- Temporary relic shutdown  
- Environmental or narrative backlash  

GM determines Strain based on:
- frequency of activation  
- failed activations  
- relic tier  
- environmental instability
