Characters pick **one** Background.  
Each provides a small mechanical perk applied through Advantage or a bonus on Aid.

---

## Wardside Survivor
Growing up in dangerous, resource-starved environments taught you to endure hardship.  
**Perk:** You gain **Advantage on FIT tests** to resist environmental harm.

---

## Outcast Wanderer
You spent years traveling alone, living off instinct and caution more than trust.  
**Perk:** Advantage on **WIS tests** to detect ambushes or signs of being followed.

---

## Sanctum Acolyte
You studied rituals, symbols, and mysteries treated as divine truths.  
**Perk:** Advantage on **INT tests** to interpret symbols, markings, and patterns.

---

## Nightwatch Scout
Trained to navigate darkness using silence and awareness.  
**Perk:** Advantage on **DEX tests** to avoid detection or move silently.

---

## Duskwood Hunter
You track elusive beasts through difficult terrain.  
**Perk:** Advantage on **WIS tests** to identify traces, tracks, or scents.

---

## Stoneforge Apprentice
Your hands are trained for precise and delicate work.  
**Perk:** Advantage on **DEX tests** involving delicate manipulation or balance.

---

## Sunken Archivist
You have a sharp mind for patterns, fragments, and lost information.  
**Perk:** Advantage on **INT tests** to assemble clues or analyze fragments.

---

## Hollow-Deep Laborer
Accustomed to tight spaces and crushing force.  
**Perk:** Advantage on **FIT tests** to push, lift, or endure cramped conditions.

---

## Twilight Healer
Trained in simple field medicine.  
**Perk:** When you use **Aid**, that ally awakens with **+1 additional HP**.

---

## Displaced Noble
Raised among emotional and political tension.  
**Perk:** Advantage on **WIS tests** to sense deception, fear, or emotion.

---

## Glimmervale Outrider
Quick reflexes refined through long travel and danger.  
**Perk:** Advantage on **DEX tests** to dodge hazards.

---

## Ruin Scholar
Fascinated with lost civilizations, devices, and symbols.  
**Perk:** Advantage on **INT tests** to understand unfamiliar objects or mechanisms.
